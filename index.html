About San Fernando

The Municipality of San Fernando is a progressive local government unit in the province of Camarines Sur, committed to serving its constituents with integrity, transparency, and excellence.

Through responsive governance and community-centered programs, we strive to improve the quality of life for all residents while promoting sustainable development and economic growth.
